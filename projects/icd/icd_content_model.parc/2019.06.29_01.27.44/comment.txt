Final updates to the CM to conform to the new (2018-19 revision of the) External Causes post-coordination model:
- Retired violenceDescriptor, and added aspectsOfAssaultAndMaltreatment instead
- Renamed contextOfAssault to contextOfAssaultAndMaltreatment
- Renamed sexOfPerpetrator to genderOfPerpetrator
